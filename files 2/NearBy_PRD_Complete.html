Product Requirements Document

NearBy — Hyperlocal Trust Commerce Platform

Version: 1.0 Status: Ready for Development Date: March 2026 Owner: Product Team Stack: React Native · Node.js · Supabase · DigitalOcean

1. Executive Summary

NearBy is a hyperlocal super app that connects customers with verified local shops — kirana stores, vegetable vendors, pharmacies, restaurants, pet stores, mobile shops, and furniture stores — for on-demand delivery powered by local delivery partners.

Unlike dark-store models (Blinkit, Zepto) that eliminate local shops, NearBy digitises and empowers them. Every order placed is income for a real shopkeeper in the customer's neighbourhood. The platform is built on trust: verified shop identities, real customer reviews tied to actual orders, and live chat with the shopkeeper before buying.

Core Thesis Dark stores win on speed. NearBy wins on trust, authenticity, and community. Our customers aren't just buying groceries — they're choosing to support Raju's vegetable shop over a faceless warehouse.

2. Problem Statement

For customers
No way to discover and order from their trusted neighbourhood shops digitally. They have to physically visit or call, can't track delivery, and have no quality guarantee.
For shop owners
Losing 20–40% of business to online aggregators and dark stores. No digital presence, no way to reach customers who've moved to apps. Existing platforms charge 18–30% commission.
For local delivery partners
No flexible earning opportunity tied to their own neighbourhood. Existing gig platforms require long commitments or cover large areas.
For the community
Local economies weakening as money flows out to centralised warehouses instead of staying in the neighbourhood ecosystem.

3. Solution

A three-sided marketplace with four apps (customer, shop owner, delivery partner, admin web dashboard) built around a trust engine that makes local shops trustworthy and discoverable at scale.

3.1 Key Differentiators

  • Verified shop identities — Aadhaar-based KYC for every shop. A verified badge tells customers this is a real, known business.
  • Trust score — Computed from verified reviews, order completion rate, response time, and KYC level. Not gameable. Powers search ranking.
  • Pre-order chat — Customers can chat with the shopkeeper before ordering. "Anna, is the chicken fresh today?" builds human trust no algorithm can replicate.
  • Shop-friendly commission — 5–8% vs 18–30% on Swiggy/Zomato. Shops keep 92–95% of every order.
  • OTP delivery confirmation — Prevents fake delivery marking. Customer controls the OTP.
  • Live inventory — Out-of-stock items disappear from search within 15 seconds of the shopkeeper toggling them off.

In Scope — V1 Customer app · Shop owner app · Delivery partner app · Admin web dashboard · Kirana, Vegetables, Pharmacy, Food/Tiffin, Pet stores, Mobile shops, Furniture shops · Hyderabad launch city · OTP login · KYC verification · Product catalogue · Order management · Live tracking · Cashfree UPI + COD payments · Trust score · Reviews · Real-time chat · Shop analytics

Vision & Strategy

Vision, Mission & Strategic Roadmap

Vision

"Every neighbourhood shop in India, discoverable and trustworthy online — while keeping money in local communities."

Mission

To build the trust infrastructure for India's 12 million local shops, starting with Hyderabad's kirana stores, vegetable vendors, and pharmacies — giving them the digital reach of Blinkit without surrendering their identity, their margins, or their customer relationships.

Strategic Pillars

Trust over speed
We don't promise 10 minutes. We promise that what you ordered is what you get, from someone you can trust. Verified shops, real reviews, human chat.
Shop-first economics
5–8% commission. No listing fees. No dark patterns to drive ad revenue. If the shop doesn't win, the platform doesn't win.
Neighbourhood network effects
Each new shop makes the platform more useful for that neighbourhood. Each new customer improves shop ratings. Self-reinforcing local loops.
Inclusive onboarding
A 60-year-old vegetable vendor without GST can join with just Aadhaar. Bulk CSV upload for pharmacies with 500 medicines. No shop left behind.

Edge Cases

Edge Cases & Handling

Every known failure mode and the system's designed response. Click any item to expand.

Order Flow Edge Cases

High Shop doesn't respond within 3 minutes
What happens
BullMQ scheduled job fires at exactly 3:00 minutes. Order status → "auto_cancelled". Cashfree refund initiated immediately. Customer gets FCM push + SMS: "Sorry, the shop didn't respond in time. Full refund initiated." Shop's response_time metric degraded — affects trust score in nightly job.
Prevention
Shop gets escalating alerts: 2:00 min remaining → 1:00 min → 0:30 sec. SMS sent at 2:00 if FCM not acknowledged. After 3 auto-cancellations in a week, admin gets alert to contact the shop owner.
High Payment succeeds but order creation fails
What happens
Cashfree webhook arrives with payment_status: SUCCESS. Order service fails due to server error. BullMQ dead-letter queue catches the failure. Admin alert fired. Payment is in "paid" state but no order exists. Manual intervention required within 30 minutes.
System design
Payment intent created BEFORE order in Supabase. Webhook handler is idempotent — can be retried 3 times. If all retries fail, Cashfree auto-refund initiated after 1 hour with customer notification. A "payment_reconciliation" BullMQ job runs every 15 minutes to detect orphaned payments.
High No delivery partner available in radius
What happens
Assignment algorithm finds no available partner within 3km. System expands radius to 5km and retries every 90 seconds for up to 10 minutes. If still no partner after 10 minutes, customer is notified: "Finding a delivery partner is taking longer than usual." Customer gets option to wait or cancel with full refund.
Prevention
Admin dashboard shows "low delivery coverage" alert when fewer than 3 partners are online in an area. Automated MSG91 SMS sent to offline partners in the area: "Orders available near you! Go online to earn."
High Customer not home — OTP delivery fails
What happens
Delivery partner arrives, customer doesn't answer. Partner taps "Customer not answering". System calls/messages customer. 3 attempts over 10 minutes. If unreachable: order escalated to admin. Partner waits up to 15 minutes. If customer confirmed they're nearby, partner waits. If not, order returned to shop and customer refunded minus delivery fee.
Special case
Customer can share OTP with a family member or security guard. OTP is valid for the entire delivery window (from pickup to 2 hours after). Customer gets SMS with OTP so they can share it if needed.
High Item out of stock discovered only when packing
What happens
Shop accepted the order but discovered an item is actually out of stock while packing. Shop opens the order and taps "Item unavailable" next to the item. Customer gets FCM notification: "Raju removed Palak from your order — not available today." Order total adjusted. Customer can cancel or continue with adjusted order within 2 minutes.
Partial refund
Cashfree partial refund initiated for removed item. Customer gets new total confirmation. If customer doesn't respond in 2 minutes, order continues with remaining items automatically (with refund for removed item).
Medium Delivery partner's app crashes mid-delivery
What happens
GPS broadcasting stops (Redis TTL expires in 30 seconds). Customer tracking map shows "Location unavailable" but order remains active. Partner reopens app — order resumes from last status. Customer gets notification: "Partner's location temporarily unavailable."
Recovery
On app reopen, partner sees "You have an active delivery" banner. Full order details restored from Supabase. GPS resumes. If partner is unreachable for more than 20 minutes during active delivery, admin alert fires.
Medium Duplicate order placed (double tap / network retry)
Prevention
Idempotency key generated client-side (UUID) and sent with order creation request. Server checks idempotency key in Redis (TTL 10 minutes). If duplicate key detected, returns existing order instead of creating new one. Payment intent is also idempotency-keyed with Cashfree.
If still duplicated
BullMQ reconciliation job checks for orders from same customer to same shop with same items within 5 minutes. Flags for admin review. Second order auto-cancelled and refunded.
Medium Shop closes mid-order (internet cuts out)
Scenario
Shop has accepted an order but their internet cuts out. They can't mark it ready. Delivery partner is waiting.
Handling
Order has a "max packing time" = estimated_ready_mins + 10 minutes. If exceeded, admin alert fires. Admin calls shop directly. If unreachable after 15 minutes, order cancelled, customer refunded, shop's completion_rate affected. Shop gets SMS when internet returns explaining what happened.
Medium Customer submits review without valid delivered order
Prevention
POST /api/v1/reviews requires order_id in body. Server validates: order exists, belongs to this customer, status is "delivered", no review already exists for this order. All 4 checks must pass. Rate limited: max 10 review attempts per hour per user.
Competitor abuse
If a competitor tries to create fake negative reviews: they'd need a real delivered order first, making mass fake reviews economically unfeasible. Suspicious review velocity (5+ reviews in an hour) triggers admin alert.
Low Cashfree webhook arrives twice (duplicate webhook)
Handling
Webhook handler checks if payment_id already processed (Supabase idempotency check). If already processed, returns 200 OK without re-processing. This is standard webhook design — Cashfree may retry on timeout.
HMAC verification
Every webhook verified with Cashfree HMAC signature before any processing. Invalid signatures return 400 and are logged for security monitoring.

Platform & Infrastructure Edge Cases

High Supabase goes down during active orders
What fails
Order status updates cannot be written. New orders cannot be placed. Active orders already in delivery continue (delivery partner uses cached data). Redis has last-known order state for active deliveries (TTL 2 hours).
Recovery
Supabase Pro has 99.9% SLA. On recovery, BullMQ replays any failed writes from the dead-letter queue. Active delivery OTPs cached in Redis — delivery confirmation still works without Supabase temporarily. Admin notified immediately via PagerDuty-equivalent (Grafana alert).
High DigitalOcean droplet goes down
Impact
Full platform unavailable. All active orders paused. Customers see connection error. Recovery time: 15–30 minutes (snapshot restore or new Droplet from Coolify backup).
Recovery plan
DO Snapshots taken weekly automatically. On outage: create new Droplet from snapshot (5 min), restore volumes from DO Spaces backup (10 min), Coolify redeploys all containers (5 min). Total RTO: ~20 minutes. Cloudflare shows maintenance page during outage.
Medium OTP SMS not delivered (MSG91 failure)
Handling
MSG91 delivery receipt checked via webhook. If "failed" status received, system retries via secondary MSG91 route (DLT alternative template). If both fail, user sees: "SMS not received? Tap to resend" with 60-second cooldown. Max 3 resends per hour per phone number.
V2 plan
Add Gupshup as backup SMS provider. Route through Gupshup if MSG91 fails on first attempt. Dual-provider setup ensures >99.9% OTP delivery rate.

Trust & Fraud Edge Cases

High Delivery partner marks order delivered without actual delivery
Prevention
OTP system prevents this — partner cannot mark delivered without customer's OTP. GPS trail shows partner's location at time of OTP entry. If location doesn't match customer's address (within 100m), flag raised for admin review. Three flags → partner suspended.
Customer recourse
Customer can report "Never received" within 24 hours of delivery. Admin investigates GPS trail. Full refund issued if fraud confirmed. Partner account suspended and earnings withheld pending investigation.
High Fake KYC documents uploaded
Detection
Admin manually reviews all documents — photo match, Aadhaar validity checks. Name on Aadhaar must match name in shop profile. Obvious inconsistencies (photoshopped images, wrong name) caught during review. In V2: DigiLocker API integration for automated Aadhaar verification.
Post-approval fraud
If fraud discovered after approval: shop immediately suspended, all pending settlements withheld, customer refunded for any active orders. Case reported to local authority if fraud amount exceeds ₹10,000.
Medium Customer disputes order but actually received it
Evidence chain
GPS trail shows partner at correct location. OTP was entered correctly (customer or family member gave OTP). Chat history may show customer confirmed arrival. Timestamps consistent.
Admin decision
Admin reviews all evidence. If GPS + OTP + timing consistent with genuine delivery, dispute rejected with explanation to customer. Three fraudulent disputes in a month → customer account flagged. Repeat behaviour → account suspension.

Supabase Database Migrations

-- Users (managed by Supabase Auth + this extension)
CREATE TABLE profiles (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY REFERENCES auth.users(id),
  phone TEXT UNIQUE NOT NULL,
  name TEXT,
  role TEXT CHECK (role IN ('customer','shop_owner','delivery','admin')) DEFAULT 'customer',
  location GEOGRAPHY(POINT,4326),
  fcm_token TEXT,
  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Shops
CREATE TABLE shops (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  owner_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id) NOT NULL,
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  category TEXT NOT NULL,
  description TEXT,
  address TEXT NOT NULL,
  geo GEOGRAPHY(POINT,4326) NOT NULL,
  hours JSONB,
  is_open BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
  is_verified BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
  kyc_status TEXT DEFAULT 'pending' CHECK (kyc_status IN ('pending','under_review','approved','rejected')),
  trust_score FLOAT DEFAULT 0,
  delivery_radius_km FLOAT DEFAULT 3,
  holiday_until TIMESTAMPTZ,
  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
);
CREATE INDEX shops_geo ON shops USING GIST(geo);
CREATE INDEX shops_category ON shops(category);

-- Products (also synced to Typesense)
CREATE TABLE products (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  shop_id UUID REFERENCES shops(id) NOT NULL,
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  category TEXT,
  price DECIMAL(10,2) NOT NULL,
  unit TEXT,
  stock_qty INT DEFAULT 0,
  is_available BOOLEAN DEFAULT TRUE,
  images TEXT[],
  deleted_at TIMESTAMPTZ,
  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Orders
CREATE TABLE orders (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  customer_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id) NOT NULL,
  shop_id UUID REFERENCES shops(id) NOT NULL,
  delivery_partner_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id),
  status TEXT DEFAULT 'pending' CHECK (status IN (
    'pending','accepted','packing','ready','assigned',
    'picked_up','out_for_delivery','delivered',
    'cancelled','auto_cancelled','rejected'
  )),
  delivery_address TEXT NOT NULL,
  delivery_geo GEOGRAPHY(POINT,4326),
  total_amount DECIMAL(10,2) NOT NULL,
  payment_mode TEXT CHECK (payment_mode IN ('upi','card','cod')),
  payment_status TEXT DEFAULT 'pending',
  payment_id TEXT,
  delivery_otp TEXT,
  estimated_ready_mins INT,
  idempotency_key UUID UNIQUE,
  placed_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW(),
  accepted_at TIMESTAMPTZ,
  delivered_at TIMESTAMPTZ,
  cancelled_at TIMESTAMPTZ
);

-- Order items
CREATE TABLE order_items (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  order_id UUID REFERENCES orders(id) NOT NULL,
  product_id UUID REFERENCES products(id) NOT NULL,
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  qty INT NOT NULL,
  unit_price DECIMAL(10,2) NOT NULL
);

-- Reviews (verified, post-delivery only)
CREATE TABLE reviews (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  order_id UUID UNIQUE REFERENCES orders(id) NOT NULL,
  reviewer_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id) NOT NULL,
  shop_id UUID REFERENCES shops(id) NOT NULL,
  rating INT CHECK (rating BETWEEN 1 AND 5) NOT NULL,
  comment TEXT,
  is_flagged BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Disputes
CREATE TABLE disputes (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  order_id UUID REFERENCES orders(id) NOT NULL,
  reporter_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id) NOT NULL,
  reason TEXT NOT NULL,
  status TEXT DEFAULT 'open' CHECK (status IN ('open','investigating','resolved')),
  resolution TEXT,
  admin_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id),
  created_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW(),
  resolved_at TIMESTAMPTZ
);

-- Row Level Security
ALTER TABLE orders ENABLE ROW LEVEL SECURITY;
CREATE POLICY "customers see own orders" ON orders FOR SELECT
  USING (customer_id = auth.uid() OR shop_id IN (
    SELECT id FROM shops WHERE owner_id = auth.uid()
  ));

Security Requirements

Security Requirements

Security is non-negotiable This platform handles real money (Cashfree payments), personal identity documents (Aadhaar), and location data. Every security measure below is mandatory for V1.

Authentication Security

RequirementImplementationPriority
OTP brute force prevention3 attempts → 10 min lock per phone number. Stored in Redis with TTL.Critical
OTP rate limitingMax 5 OTP requests per phone per hour. Prevents SMS bombing.Critical
JWT signingHS256 with 256-bit random secret. Separate secret for refresh tokens.Critical
JWT short expiryAccess token: 30 days. Refresh token: 90 days in Redis. Revocable.Critical
Role enforcementEvery API route has role guard middleware. Admin role never returned to clients.Critical
Supabase RLSRow-level security ensures cross-user data access impossible even if API bypassed.Critical

Payment Security

RequirementImplementation
Server-side price calculationOrder total always calculated from database product prices — never from client request body. Client sends only product IDs and quantities.
Cashfree webhook HMACEvery webhook verified with Cashfree secret key before processing. Invalid signatures return 400, logged for security monitoring.
Idempotency keysPayment intents and order creation use idempotency keys — duplicate requests return existing results, never create duplicates.
PCI complianceNo card data ever touches NearBy servers. Cashfree SDK handles all card input. We are not in PCI scope.
Settlement audit trailEvery commission, payout, and refund logged in Supabase with Cashfree transaction ID.

Data Security

RequirementImplementation
KYC document storageAadhaar and GST docs stored in Cloudflare R2 private bucket. Only accessible via time-limited signed URLs (5 minutes). Never publicly accessible.
GPS data retentionLive GPS stored in Redis with 30s TTL — not persisted to Supabase. Historical GPS stored only for active disputes (30 days, then deleted).
Data minimisationCustomer phone number hashed before logging. Aadhaar number never stored — only document image reference in R2.
Encryption in transitAll traffic via HTTPS (Cloudflare TLS 1.3). Internal services on private DO VPC network.
Encryption at restSupabase encrypts at rest. Cloudflare R2 encrypts at rest. Redis data: sensitive fields encrypted with AES-256 before storage.

Infrastructure Security

LayerControls
Cloudflare (edge)DDoS mitigation, bot detection, IP reputation blocking, 100 WAF rules, rate limiting at edge
NginxOnly Cloudflare IPs whitelisted. Direct IP access blocked. Request size limit 10MB. Server headers hidden.
Node.js APIHelmet.js headers. CORS restricted to app domains. Input validation with Joi on every endpoint. SQL injection impossible (Supabase parameterised queries).
DigitalOceanFirewall rules: only ports 80, 443 publicly accessible. SSH restricted to admin IPs. DO VPC for internal service communication.
Secrets managementAll secrets in .env files, never in code. .env in .gitignore. Coolify manages env vars securely for containers.

Common Support Scenarios

Scenario: "I paid but my order was never confirmed"

  1. Check Supabase orders table for order matching customer phone + recent timestamp
  2. Check Supabase payments table for payment_status
  3. If payment_status = paid but no order: orphaned payment — run reconciliation manually, create order or refund
  4. If payment_status = pending: Cashfree may still be processing — wait 5 minutes, check again
  5. Contact customer with outcome within 30 minutes

Scenario: "Shop says they didn't receive order notification"

  1. Check Supabase order status — if "pending" for >3 minutes, check BullMQ dead-letter queue
  2. Check FCM delivery logs — was the notification sent?
  3. Check if shop's FCM token is valid (may have reinstalled app)
  4. Check if MSG91 SMS was sent as backup
  5. If shop still didn't receive: manually call them with order details. Update their FCM token if needed.

Scenario: "Delivery partner says customer gave wrong OTP"

  1. Check Supabase orders table — delivery_otp field
  2. Check if customer's phone number is correct (may have changed)
  3. Admin can see OTP and relay it to partner via phone call
  4. If customer is unreachable: follow "Customer not home" escalation path
  5. Log incident — if pattern repeats with same customer, flag account
